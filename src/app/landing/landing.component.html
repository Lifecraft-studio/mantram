About Mantram

MANTRAM INDUSTRIES PRIVATE LIMITED (Formally Known As Madhu Cable and Conductor Pvt. Ltd.) is an ISO 9001 Certified company started in 2016 in Indore. We manufacture under a well-known and accepted brand name - "MANTRAM" in the cable & conductor industry. We manufacture large range of products in LT PVC/XLPE Power Cables, Control Cables, AB Cables & AAAC, ACSR Conductors.

In the field of power distribution segment, MANTRAM is the synonymous in cable and conductor field and we have been enjoying faith and reputation from our esteemed customers due to our product quality as well as service. The entire range of our product basket is made with our state of the art technology. Till now we have no plan to have collaboration any technology for our existing product range. Our quality standard is ensured by stringent quality check both on incoming raw materials up to finished products based on our well framed quality assurance standard with continuous developmental R&D activity both for raw material and finished products.
